Frequently asked questions
How does the 30-day free trial work?
Every new account automatically gets a full 30-day Pro trial — no credit card needed. You'll have access to unlimited scans, all findings, PDF reports, AI analysis, and CVE lookup. After 30 days, if you don't upgrade, the account moves to the Free plan automatically. You can upgrade to Pro at any time for $1.99/month to keep all Pro features.
Can I try Pro before paying?
Yes — we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Sign up, try every Pro feature, and if it's not for you we'll refund in full.
What's the difference between Basic AI and Deep AI?
Basic AI gives you a summary of detected risks. Deep AI (Pro's AI-Only mode) runs a full reasoning pass over your code — it explains the attack surface, maps each finding to OWASP categories, predicts severity, lists remediation steps with code examples, and assesses compliance impact.
What counts as a "scan"?
Each code snippet analysis or AI-Only analysis is one scan. Repo scans count as one scan regardless of repository size. Limits reset at midnight UTC.
How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed once per year at a 20% discount. For Pro that's $1.59/mo (billed as $19.08/year). You can cancel before renewal for a pro-rated refund.
I'm a developer testing my own app — do I need Pro?
If you're a developer testing via the API or a local environment with DEV_MODE enabled, all features are unlocked for testing purposes. Pro is required for production access to repo scanning, deep AI, and CVE enrichment.
Can I upgrade or downgrade later?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle — you keep Pro access until then.
